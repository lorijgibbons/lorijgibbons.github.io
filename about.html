ALL ABOUT ME

Hello! I'm
Lori.

I am a designer who sweats every detail of every pixel on the screen, each Pantone shade used, and the correct pronunciation of the word, “gif.” I am an experienced, technically, and classically trained visual designer, perpetually striving to ideate and collaborate to bring imaginings to life.

SERVICES

What I Can Create for You

I aim to team up with great people with purposeful ideas to work on creative projects. I believe great design can dramatically impact the world - it can evoke emotion, inspire awe, empower social movements, transcend cultural boundaries, or even alter world thinking. Here are some of the things I do:

  • Creative Direction

    I strive to make each project a success by creating organized workflows and motivating teams to make the best products through rigorous reviews. Needless to say, my team stays on track and my clients always know where we are in the process.

  • Brand Identity

    Paula Scher said “Words have meaning, type has spirit.” I believe brands are the spirit - they build loyalty and create value over time. I love to create brands identities that resonate with audiences and get responses.

  • Web Design + UI Design

    My objective is to ease user flow through interfaces by carefully and thoughtfully crafted experiences. Design should not only look good to show a polished brand, but should function seamlessly across all devices and platforms.

  • Graphic Design

    Since CS3, Ive been pushing pixels with Adobe products and it keeps getting even more interesting the more the years go by. As long as I am creating graphics that blow your mind, I am a happy gal.

  • Illustration + Artwork

    I love to let my creative juices flow and create whimsical illustrations, acrylic paintings, or digital works of art. I like to try new mediums all the time - which keeps my hands full with all the possibilities!

PURPOSE AND GOALS

Design Aspirations

MY MISSION

I make memorable designs that will change an idea, product, or service (whatever it may be!) into a success. I love to build recognizable brands; beat the competition; make connections; begin change; explore ideas. I make visual identities and designs that change brand perceptions while looking great across platforms and mediums.

MY VISION

I envision building communities that collaborate on design ideas and work on projects to bring visions to life. I strive to take ideas and transform them into a visual reality. When great ideas are coupled with great design and then spread to the right audience, change happens.
