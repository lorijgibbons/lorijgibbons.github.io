BRANDING + WEB DESIGN
Paterson Industries
Client: Clarence Paterson
Year: 2019
Role: Web Designer, Graphic Designer
Since their inception in 2014, Paterson Industries had no website presence. I was pleased to work with a team to develop a full communications campaign with many assets, including this website.
ABOUT THIS PROJECT

This website style conveys a clean aesthetic with simple headings and dynamic real-world images to attract a humble, salt-of-the-earth customer.

Paterson Industries, an oilfield services startup, specializes in installing, assembling, fabricating, maintaining, and repairing mechanical piping systems. The client needed a web presence to compete with other services in the area. To meet this need, I created an entire communications campaign to launch the client into the next phase of the company's growth. View the entire communications campaign here!

I worked with a design team to create a brand persona and identity complete with a logo, colors and brand manifesto. After the creative brief, I created a website in WordPress as well as sample social media ads to serve as inspiration for future design developments. My team also performed user testing on the website to optimize user experience and increase conversion rate.

PROJECT GOALS

The goals of this site include competitor differentiation and audience exposure, which will lead to a robust client base.

Final Result

Not only was a responsive WordPress website created to launch the client to the next phase of the business goals, but a logo, branding package, and communications campaign were created as well.
