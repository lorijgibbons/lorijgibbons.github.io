WEB DESIGN
Distance & Continuing Education
Client: UF Teaching and Technology
Year: 2019
Role: Web Designer, Graphic Designer
DCE needed a modernized web interface to appeal to young, tech-savvy students who are interested in pursuing various forms of online education with UF.
ABOUT THIS PROJECT

The office of Distance Learning has a long history with UF and has many departments and services to offer online students and faculty.

I wanted a dynamic interface to capture attention so I created a video header. Dynamic elements were chosen to make multi-dimensional services seem simple and relevant, including sortable service tiles, a testimonial section, and a video header.

To create a connection with viewers and show that there are people behind the facade of distance education, I wanted a rich “Meet Us” page with photographs and bios.

PROJECT GOALS

I wanted the services, mission and purpose of DCE to be clear to viewers.

Final Result

I achieved project goals by creating a friendly site that shows there are people working hard behind the scenes interested in student success.
